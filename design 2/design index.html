Recipy Book

The concept is to design a typographic recipe book tailored for teenagers, students, and professionals living away from home. This book is ideal for those who return late from college or work and need quick and easy recipes for lunch or dinner. It aims to provide a sense of comfort and nostalgia, recreating the warmth of home-cooked meals while being practical and user-friendly.
